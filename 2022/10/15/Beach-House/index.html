Once Twice Melody

from Once Twice Melody (2022)

Out in the summer sun

夏日鼎盛

She was the only one

她孑然一身

Days go by, in her eyes

时光流逝，尽收她眼底

Belle de jour in front of me

我的白日美人

 

No matter where you go

你去哪呢

There’ll always be your shadow

这里总有你的阴影

Lalala, days go by, lalala, in her eyes

时光流逝，尽收她眼底

Once, twice, melody

一遍，两遍的旋律

 

The purple on the vine

藤上的紫

The velveteen tree line

天鹅绒般的柔荫

Nights fly by, in her mind

长夜已逝，如梦幻影

All along the boulevard

在她脑海中疾行

 

She tries to understand

她欲知晓

A never never land

乌有之乡

Lalala, across thе room, lalala, sweet perfume

满屋是香水甜味

The sun, thе moon, the raindrops too

太阳，月亮，和雨滴

 

Space Song

from Depression Cherry (2015)

夜已深

You held on tight

你紧握不放

换取你的微笑

Somewhere in these eyes

在他人眼光中

I’m on your side

我在你身旁

你瞪大眼睛

You get it right

如你所想

 

Fall back into place

落回远处吧

Were you ever lost?

你也迷失过吗

Was she ever found?

她被找到了吗

Somewhere in these eyes

在他人眼光中

 

Fall back into place

落回远处吧
